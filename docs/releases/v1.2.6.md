# DeskBox 1.2.6

## English

DeskBox 1.2.6 focuses on Todo reminder reliability and Quick Capture responsiveness. It keeps the 1.2 series stable while making the two feature widgets feel smoother in daily use.

### Highlights

- Todo reminders now support per-task reminder offsets, snooze recovery after app restart, and safer overdue reminder handling.
- Native Windows notifications can now complete a Todo item or delay it from the reminder flow.
- Recurring Todo completion history is folded under the active recurring task, keeping the main list cleaner.
- Todo and Quick Capture multi-select behavior is more complete, including rectangle selection, formatted copy, drag-out text, and Escape-to-clear.
- Quick Capture tab switching is smoother thanks to delayed refresh work, reduced UI-thread churn, stable empty-state layout, and a softer content transition.
- Quick Capture automatic clipboard capture now defaults to off, and disabling Quick Capture also disables automatic clipboard and image capture.

### 更新重点

- 待办提醒支持每条任务独立提醒偏移，稍后提醒可在 DeskBox 重启后恢复，逾期提醒逻辑也更稳。
- 系统通知支持直接完成待办，或在提醒流程中选择稍后提醒。
- 重复待办的已完成历史会折叠在当前重复任务下方，主列表不会被重复完成记录撑得越来越长。
- 待办和随记的多选能力更完整，支持框选、格式化复制、拖拽导出文本和 Esc 取消选择。
- 随记 Tab 切换更顺滑：延迟较重的刷新工作，减少 UI 线程压力，稳定空状态布局，并使用更自然的内容过渡动画。
- 随记自动复制默认关闭；关闭随记功能时，也会联动关闭自动复制和图片复制。

### 测试建议

- 检查默认、单条自定义、稍后提醒等待办提醒场景，并验证重启 DeskBox 后提醒状态是否恢复。
- 检查系统通知里的完成和稍后提醒动作。
- 检查重复待办完成历史的折叠展示。
- 在随记存在大量文本和图片记录、以及空状态时切换 Tab，确认不卡顿、不跑位。
- 检查待办和随记的框选、格式化复制、拖拽导出文本和 Esc 取消选择。
- 确认 Direct 版关于页显示 1.2.6，Store 包版本为 1.2.6.0。
